## ğŸ’¬ How to Use

### Starting a Direct Conversation

1. **Open Chat Panel**
   - Click the chat button (bottom-right corner)

2. **Click "New Chat"**
   - This shows a list of all users in the system

3. **Search for a User**
   - Type in the search box to find someone
   - Users are labeled as Admin, Evaluator, or User

4. **Start Chatting**
   - Click on a user to open a direct message conversation
   - Type your message and press Enter or click Send

---

### Creating a Group Chat

1. **Open Chat Panel**
   - Click the chat button

2. **Click "New Group"**
   - Enter a name for your group
   - Example: "Math Evaluators" or "Grade 3 Team"

3. **Add Members**
   - Search for users
   - Click on users to add them to the group
   - Selected users appear as green tags above the list
   - Click the Ã— on a tag to remove someone

4. **Create the Group**
   - Click "Create Group (#)" button at the bottom
   - The number shows how many members you've selected

5. **Start Messaging**
   - The group conversation will open automatically
   - All members can see all messages

---

### Sending Messages

#### In Any Conversation:
1. Type your message in the text field at the bottom
2. Press **Enter** or click the **Send button** (paper plane icon)
3. Your message appears immediately
4. Messages from others appear on the left (gray bubbles)
5. Your messages appear on the right (blue bubbles)

#### Message Details:
- **Sender name** and **role badge** appear above each message (for others)
- **Timestamp** shows when the message was sent
- **Group chats** show participant count in the header

---

### Reading Messages

1. Click on any conversation in the left sidebar
2. Conversation opens with all messages
3. **Unread messages are automatically marked as read** when you view them
4. **Scroll up** to see older messages
5. New messages appear at the bottom automatically

## â“ FAQs

### Can I delete messages?
Currently, messages cannot be deleted. Be mindful of what you send.

### Can I edit messages?
Message editing is not currently available.

### Can I send files or images?
Currently, only text messages are supported.

### How do I leave a group?
Group leaving functionality is not yet available. Contact an admin if needed.

### Can I see if someone read my message?
Read receipts are tracked but not currently displayed in the UI.

### How often do messages update?
- Conversations list: Every 5 seconds when panel is open
- Unread count: Every 10 seconds
- Messages: Auto-refresh when viewing a conversation

### Who can see my messages?
- **Direct messages**: Only you and the recipient
- **Group chats**: All group members
- **Broadcasts**: All users in the selected target group
